Living with Diabetes

Your Daily Management & Wellness Resource

Diabetes management depends on your specific type, but everyone benefits from the same core pillars: balanced nutrition, regular activity, and careful monitoring.

1. Type 1 Diabetes

Individuals require lifelong insulin therapy and careful blood sugar monitoring.

Recommended Diet

  • Balanced meals / Controlled carbs
  • Whole grains (oats, brown rice)
  • Lean proteins & healthy fats
  • Vegetables with every meal

Exercise

  • Walking & Swimming
  • Light jogging / Cycling

2. Type 2 Diabetes

Linked strongly to lifestyle; focuses on improving insulin sensitivity.

Recommended Diet

  • High-fiber (legumes, veggies)
  • Low Glycemic Index foods
  • Reduce sugar & processed foods

Exercise

  • Brisk walking (most effective)
  • Strength training
  • Yoga (150 mins/week goal)

3. Gestational Diabetes

Occurs during pregnancy; regular monitoring is essential.

  • Small, frequent meals / High-fiber
  • Lean proteins and dairy
  • Prenatal yoga & light stretching

General Guidelines

Healthy Eating: Avoid excessive sugar, stay hydrated, and maintain consistent meal timings.

Physical Activity: Improves insulin sensitivity, weight control, and overall mood.

Positive Living

Diabetes does not prevent you from living a full, active, and meaningful life. With proper care, awareness, and consistency, you can maintain your normal daily routines including work, travel, and sports.

🚨 Medical Requirement

A doctor is a must. Diabetes management is highly individual.

Advice may vary based on your age, health condition, and current medications. Always follow your healthcare provider’s instructions strictly. Do not rely solely on general online advice.
